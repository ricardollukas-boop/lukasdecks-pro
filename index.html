What to ask testers

Ask deck builders to test one real estimate, one project, and the Deck Profit calculator. The best feedback is what they would actually use on a job site.

Recommended test questions: Is the app simple? Are the deck prices useful? Is anything missing? Would you pay for this if it saved time?
